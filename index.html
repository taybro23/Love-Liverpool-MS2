Places Of Interest



World Museum

Visit the World Museum in Liverpool for a look into the past. With an extensive collection of exhibits including natural history, human history and astronomy, there are hours of fun to be had here!


Walker Art Gallery

The impressive Walker Art Gallery is a must see when visiting the amazing city of Liverpool. Located inside of the World Museum, the artworks on show date as far back as the 1300s!


LFC & EFC Stadiums

There are two massive football clubs in Liverpool; Liverpool FC and Everton FC! Whichever team you support, both have iconic stadiums that are a joy to experience.
