Places Of Interest

World Museum

World Museum
William Brown St
Liverpool
L3 8EN
Tel: 0151 478 4393

View Website

Visit the World Museum in Liverpool for a look into the past. With an extensive collection of exhibits including natural history, human history and astronomy, there are hours of fun to be had here!

Walker Art Gallery

Walker Art Gallery
William Brown St
Liverpool
L3 8EN
Tel: 0151 478 4199

View Website

The impressive Walker Art Gallery is a must see when visiting the amazing city of Liverpool. Located inside of the World Museum, the artworks on show date as far back as the 1300s!

Liverpool Cathedral

Liverpool Cathedral
St James' Mount
Liverpool
L1 7AZ
Tel: 0151 709 9722

View Website

Built in 1904, the stunning Liverpool Cathedral is a must see when visiting the city of Liverpool. The Cathedral is Britains largest, and the 5th largest in Europe!

Liverpool One

With a wide variety of shops and restaurants for all tastes and budgets, Liverpool One is the place to shop and dine! You'll find your high-street favourites, as well as your high end favourites. Once you're done there, you can relax at one of many top quality hotels in and around Liverpool One!
